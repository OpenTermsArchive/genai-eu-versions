**I. Types Of Cookies**

Cookies are small text files stored in your computing or other electronic devices when you visit our Services.

Cookies can be “persistent” or “session” cookies. Persistent cookies may remain on your personal computer or mobile device across browsing sessions, while session cookies are generally deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

We use both first party and third party cookies for several reasons. Cookies set by the website owner (in this case Qwen) are called "first party cookies". Only the website owner can access the first party cookies it sets. Third party cookies are placed by third-party providers. The parties that set these third-party cookies can recognise your device both when it visits the Services and also when it visits other websites that have partnered with them.

Some cookies are required for technical reasons that are strictly necessary for our Services to operate, and we refer to these as "Strictly Necessary" cookies. Other cookies also enable us to provide Services functionality, or to enhance visitors' experience as described in more detail below.

Certain sections of our Services may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs) that permit us, for example, to count users who have visited those pages and understand how our Services are used (for example, to assess the popularity of certain pages and services) and to improve the performance and reliability of our Services. For simplicity, we also refer to these technologies as "Cookies" in this Cookie Notice.
